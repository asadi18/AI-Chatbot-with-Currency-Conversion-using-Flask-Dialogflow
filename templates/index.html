AquaBasic

AquaBasic is a simple chatbot built as a practice project. It can convert USD and Euro to BDT and handle basic small talk. While this version is limited, it proves how such bots can be expanded into full-featured assistants for any application.

What AquaBasic Can Do:

  • Convert USD to BDT
  • Convert EUR to BDT
  • Answer basic questions like "Who are you?"
  • Talk about cricket, weather, and more
  • Respond to "Help me", "What's your name?" and similar prompts
